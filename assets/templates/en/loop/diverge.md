# Phase 2 — Diverge

## Task
Generate N candidates for possible next steps. At least 5, at most 12. Different lines — not all in the same direction. At least one explicit "do nothing" candidate is allowed and sometimes wise.

## Mandatory Inputs
- output of this loop's Observe phase
- active standing concerns (`stimuli/standing/`)
- current capabilities and tools (inventory)

## Mandatory Output
`corpus/episodic/loop-{NNNNN}-diverge.md`, at least 10 substantial lines, containing:
- a numbered candidate list
- per candidate: a short description, presumed benefit, presumed effort
- marking of the "do nothing" candidate if present
- diversity note: in what respect do the candidates differ?

## SP Reference
SC-001 (phase discipline). No flattening of the candidates — ensure diversity.

## Model
Sonnet — broad generation.
